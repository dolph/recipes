# Breakfast ramen

In a small mixing bowl, whisk together until homogenous:

- 1 tablespoon **peanut butter**
- 1 tablespoon **chili sauce**, such as sriracha or sambal oolek
- 1 tablespoon **shoyu sauce**
- 1 teaspoon **oyster sauce**
- 1/2 teaspoon **fish sauce**

Toss to coat:

- 1 portion **ramen-style noodles**, cooked

Top:

- 1 scrambled **egg**

Optional garnishes:

- Thinly sliced **scallion**, green parts only
- Minced **cilantro**
- **Toasted sesame oil**
- **Everything bagel seasoning** (or just sesame seeds)
- **XO sauce**
