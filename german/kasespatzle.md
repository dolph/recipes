# Käsespätzle

## Spätzle

Prepare a pot of salted, boiling water.

In a medium bowl, mix:

- 2.5 cups **flour**
- 1.5 tsp **kosher salt**

Add and beat until elastic and glossy.

- 4 large **eggs**
- 3/4 cup **whole milk**

Rest 10-15 minutes.

Press batter into salted boiling water. When they float, scoop into ice water, drain well, and set aside.

## Combine

Slowly caramelize until deeply golden.

- 2 large **yellow onions**, thinly sliced
- 4 tbsp **unsalted butter**

Add, toss to combine, and cover until melted:

- Spätzle
- 6 oz **Emmentaler**, grated
- 4 oz **Gruyère**, grated
- 2 oz **Comté**, grated
- Freshly-grated **nutmeg**
- **Black pepper**
