# Gurkensalat

In a medium bowl, toss to coat:

- 2 large **English (or Persian) cucumbers**, thinly sliced
- 1 teaspoon **kosher salt**

Let sit 10–15 minutes, and then gently squeeze out and discard excess liquid.

Meanwhile, in a small bowl, whisk together:

- 3 tablespoons **white wine vinegar**
- 2 tablespoons **neutral oil**
- 1/2 teaspoon **sugar**
- **Black pepper**
- 1–2 tablespoons finely sliced **dill** (or **chives**)
- 1 teaspoon finely minced **shallot** (optional)

Toss to thoroughly coat:

- Drained cucumbers
- Dressing
