# Creami

## Strawberry ice cream

In a large bowl, add and mash:

- 8 ounces **fresh strawberries**, trimmed, cut in quarters
- 1/2 cup **sugar**
- 1 teaspoon **light corn syrup**
- 1 teaspoon **lemon juice**

Allow mixture to sit for 10 minutes, stirring regularly.

Add, and mix until well combined:

- 1 cup **heavy cream**

Transfer to storage cup and freeze for 24 hours.

Process on _ICE CREAM_ with the _CREAMi_ paddle.

## Frosted Lemonade Ice Cream

Blend with an immersion blender in a storage cup:

- 14 ounces **Premier Protein shake (vanilla)**
- 1 tablespoon **lemon instant pudding mix**
- 2 teaspoons **lemon zest**

Freeze for 24 hours.

Select _SCOOP_, and process on _CREAMIFIT_ with the _Creamerizer_ paddle.

## Berry sorbet

Add to CREAMi pint, firmly pressing down:

- 3 cups **mixed fruit**

Freeze for 24 hours.

Install _Creamerizer_ paddle, and process on _SORBET_.

## Vanilla gelato

Place into a small saucepan and whisk until fully combined and sugar is dissolved:

- 4 large **egg yolks**
- 1 tablespoon **light corn syrup**
- 1/4 cup + 1 tablespoon **sugar**

Add and stir to combine:

- 1 cup **heavy cream**
- 2/3 cup **whole milk**
- 1 whole **vanilla bean**, split in half lengthwise, scraped

Place saucepan on stove over medium heat, stirring constantly with a rubber spatula. Cook until temperature reaches 165-175 F.

Remove from heat, and transfer to CREAMi pint (optionally, strain).

Freeze for 24 hours.

Install _Creamerizer_ paddle and process on _GELATO_.

## Triple chocolate ice cream

In a microwave-safe bowl, add:

- 1 tablespoon **cream cheese**
- 2 tablespoons **cocoa powder**

Microwave for 10 seconds. Add and mix to combine until the mixture looks like frosting:

- 1/3 cup **sugar**
- 1 teaspoon **vanilla extract**

Slowly add and mix until fully combined and dissolved:

- 3/4 cup **heavy cream**
- 1 cup **whole milk**

Transfer to CREAMi pint, and freeze for 24 hours.

Install _Creamerizer_ paddle, and process on _ICE CREAM_.

Create a hole through the center of the pint, and add:

- 2 tablespoons **mini chocolate chips**
- 2 tablespoons **brownie chunks**

Process on _MIX-IN_.

## Cookies & cream

In a microwave-safe bowl, add:

- 1 tablespoon **cream cheese**

Microwave for 10 seconds. Add and mix to combine until the mixture looks like frosting:

- 1/3 cup **granulated sugar**
- 1 teaspoon **vanilla extract**

Slowly add and mix until fully combined and dissolved:

- 3/4 cup **heavy cream**
- 1 cup **whole milk**

Transfer to CREAMi pint, and freeze for 24 hours.

Install _Creamerizer_ paddle, and process on _ICE CREAM_.

Create a hole through the center of the pint, and add:

- 3 **Oreos**
