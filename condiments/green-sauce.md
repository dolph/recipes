# Green sauce

Blend until smooth:

- 3 **serranos**, stems removed
- 1 **avocado**
- 1/4 cup **mayo**
- 1/4 cup **sour cream**
- 1 bunch **cilantro**, some stems removed
- 1 bunch **green onion**, roots removed
- 1 tablespoon **apple cider vinegar**
- Salt, pepper, garlic powder, MSG blend

Transfer to a squirt bottle, and refrigerate up to 3 days.

Serve with:

- **Pulled pork**, **cilantro**, **pickled red onion**, **cotija**, & **corn tortillas**
