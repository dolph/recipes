# Rack of lamb

Season:

- 1 **rack of lamb**
- Kosher salt
- Black pepper

Sear on high heat (~2 min per side).

Blend:

- 6 **garlic** cloves
- 1 cup **breadcrumbs**
- 0.5 cups **parsley**
- 0.5 cups **parmesean**

Coat lamb:

- **Yellow mustard**

Roll to coat:

- Blended mixture

Blend, and strain into a spray bottle to make a spritzer:

- 3 cups **mint leaves**
- 2 cups water

Smoke at 275 F to 140 F internal, spritzing 2-3 times.

Garnish:

- **Balsamic vinegar**
- **Mint leaves**
