# Grilled shrimp

Marinate for 15-30 minutes:

- 1 lb shell-on **shrimp**
- **Olive oil**, to coat
- Zest of 1 **lemon**, juice reserved
- 2 cloves **garlic**, grated
- Kosher salt
- Black pepper

Grill on both sides over high heat until firm, about 60-90 seconds per side.

Toss with:

- **Parsley**, minced
- Juice of 1 lemon
