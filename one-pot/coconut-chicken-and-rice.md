# Coconut chicken & rice

Heat oven to 375 degrees.

Rub and season chicken:

- 1.5 pounds **boneless, skinless chicken thighs**, each thigh cut into 2 equal-size pieces
- **Neutral oil**
- **Kosher salt**
- **Black pepper**

Preheat a large Dutch oven over medium heat:

- 2 tablespoons **neutral oil**

Working in two batches, brown chicken, turning halfway, until no longer pink, around 5 minutes per batch. Transfer to a plate.

Add, the ginger and stir until fragrant:

- 1 tablespoon **neutral oil**
- 2 tablespoons **minced **fresh ginger**
- 1 tablespoon **minced garlic**

Add and stir until evenly coated in the oil:

- 1.5 cups **short-grain white rice**, rinsed until water runs clear

Add and stir to lift up any browned bits on the bottom of the pot:

- 1.75 cups **low-sodium chicken broth**
- 1 (13.5-ounce) can **full-fat coconut milk**
- 1 **yellow bell pepper**, cored, seeded and chopped
- ½ cup **roasted cashews**, coarsely chopped
- 3 **scallions**, green and white parts, thinly sliced
- **Kosher salt**
- **Black pepper**

Arrange chicken on top, add any accumulated juices from the plate and bring to a boil over high:

- Chicken with juices

Cover and bake until all of the liquid is absorbed, rice is tender and chicken is cooked through (~25 minutes).

Garnish:

- 2 tablespoons coarsely chopped **cilantro**

Serve with:

- **Hot sauce**
